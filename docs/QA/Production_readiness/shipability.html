Section 1 — Must-Pass Test Cases
🏗 1. Build & Stability 8 tests ▼
TC-001
Clean Build — Zero Warnings
Run xcodebuild -scheme workout -destination 'platform=iOS Simulator,name=iPhone 17 Pro' build. Must complete without errors. Warnings are acceptable only if pre-existing and documented.
Pass: Exit code 0. Zero new errors. Zero new build-time warnings added by recent commits.
TC-002
No Crash on Cold Start — Simulator
Fresh install on iPhone 17 Pro simulator. Launch app. Wait 5 seconds. Verify SeedStore.isLoaded = true and home screen renders completely.
Pass: App reaches home screen in <2s. No EXC_BAD_ACCESS, SIGABRT, or watchdog timeout. isLoaded transitions to true.
TC-003
No Crash on Any Tab Navigation
Launch app with seeded data. Tap each of the 6 tabs in sequence (Home, Trainer, Workout, Progress, StrengthLab, Settings). Hold 2 seconds per tab. Repeat 3 cycles.
Pass: No crash on any tab. All 6 tabs render their content. No blank screens.
TC-004
Background / Foreground Stability
Open app. Background it (Home button). Wait 10 seconds. Return to foreground. Repeat 10 times. Also test with active workout in progress.
Pass: No crash across 10 background/foreground cycles. Active workout state preserved on return. No ANR on foreground transition.
TC-005
Memory Pressure Stability
Open app with 50+ session data seed. Open heavy views (StrengthLab, Progress charts). Simulate memory warning via Xcode Debug > Simulate Memory Warning.
Pass: App handles memory warning without crash. Data not corrupted. Caches can be rebuilt on next analyticsRefresh call.
TC-006
Data Persistence Across Restarts
Log a complete workout session. Force-quit app. Relaunch. Verify: session appears in Progress tab, e1RM is present in StrengthLab, lastPerformanceCache reflects the session.
Pass: All logged data survives app kill and relaunch. No data loss. isLoaded sets to true within 2s of relaunch.
TC-007
Simulator Build — No Device-Only APIs
Confirm all simulator builds succeed without device-only framework conditionals (#if targetEnvironment) that would break simulator runs. HealthKit stubs must be functional.
Pass: All features accessible on simulator. No compile-time errors for simulator target. HealthKit gracefully absent on simulator.
TC-008
Physical Device Build and Launch
Build and run on a real iPhone (any model). Verify HealthKit permission is requested correctly, Apple Watch integration initializes, and all 6 tabs load.
Pass: Device build succeeds. HealthKit permission dialog appears. No crash on real hardware that didn't occur on simulator.
🧮 2. Data Accuracy 12 tests ▼
TC-009
e1RM — Epley Formula (2–10 reps)
Log 100 kg × 5 reps. Expected Epley e1RM: 100 × (1 + 5/30) = 116.67 kg. Log 80 kg × 10 reps. Expected: 80 × (1 + 10/30) = 106.67 kg. Verify in StrengthLab.
Pass: Displayed e1RM within ±0.5 kg of manual calculation. Formula uses Epley for reps 2–10.
TC-010
e1RM — Mayhew Formula (11–15 reps)
Log 70 kg × 12 reps. Mayhew formula: weight / (0.522 + 0.419 × e^(-0.055 × reps)). Verify app uses Mayhew at reps = 11 and Epley at reps = 10 (boundary test).
Pass: Formula switches at exactly 11 reps. Mayhew result within ±0.5 kg of manual. Epley used at 10, Mayhew at 11.
TC-011
e1RM — Exact (1 rep), Excluded (>15 reps)
Log 150 kg × 1 rep. e1RM should equal exactly 150 kg. Log 60 kg × 16 reps. e1RM should return 0 (excluded, not calculated).
Pass: 1-rep e1RM = weight exactly. >15 reps e1RM = 0 (excluded, not causing analytics issues).
TC-012
Readiness Score Range (20–99)
Seed scenarios producing minimum readiness (consecutive high-RPE days) and maximum readiness (7-day rest, good sleep). Verify score stays within 20–99 range in all conditions.
Pass: Score never exceeds 99, never falls below 20, regardless of log data. Score of 0 or 100 indicates a range clamp bug.
TC-013
Equipment Weight Calculation — Dumbbell ×2
Log dumbbell curl at 20 kg. The displayed "total load" should be 40 kg (bilateral). Equipment.effectiveWeight should apply the ×2 multiplier. Verify in StrengthLab e1RM.
Pass: Dumbbell entries use 2× the logged weight for bilateral total. e1RM calculated on total load, not per-hand weight.
TC-014
Equipment Weight Calculation — Barbell 20 kg Floor
Log barbell squat at 0 kg (empty bar). Equipment.effectiveWeight should floor at 20 kg (bar weight). Logging "20 kg" on barbell should mean 40 kg total (20 bar + 20 added). Verify calculation.
Pass: Empty barbell (0 kg entered) = 20 kg effective weight. 20 kg entered on barbell = 40 kg effective. Floor enforced consistently.
TC-015
WorkoutLog Persistence — All Fields Saved
Complete a workout with: drop sets, RPE values, toFailure flag, supersetGroup assignment. Force-quit and relaunch. Verify all fields present in the reloaded WorkoutLogEntry.
Pass: isDropCompleted, dropWeight, dropReps, toFailure, rpe, velocityProfile, supersetGroup all preserved across kill+relaunch cycle.
TC-016
Cardio Log Persistence
Complete a cardio session (run: 30 min, 5 km). Force-quit and relaunch. Verify cardio session appears in Progress tab with correct duration and distance values.
Pass: Cardio session persists with correct type, duration, distance, and date across relaunch.
TC-017
Streak Calculation Accuracy
Seed sessions on days: Mon, Wed, Fri (week 1), Mon, Wed, Fri (week 2). Verify streak = 6 (not calendar days). Skip Saturday. Start Sunday. Verify streak definition matches documented behavior.
Pass: Streak count matches number of training sessions, not calendar days. Streak definition consistent with Progress tab display and homeCache.
TC-018
Personal Records Detection
Log bench press: session 1 = 80 kg × 5 (e1RM ~93), session 2 = 85 kg × 5 (e1RM ~99). Verify session 2 is flagged as PR. Then log 85 kg × 4 reps. Verify it does NOT override the previous PR.
Pass: PRs based on e1RM, not raw weight. PR correctly attributed to highest e1RM session. PR not retroactively overwritten by lower e1RM performance.
TC-019
RPE Range Validation (6–10 Only)
Attempt to save RPE = 5 (below range) and RPE = 11 (above range). These must be rejected or not be selectable. Confirm only values 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 are available.
Pass: RPE selector enforces 6–10 range. Values outside range are not accessible or produce a validation error. Stored RPE is always nil or 6–10.
TC-020
Date Calculations — Timezone Safety
Log a session at 11:45 PM. Log another at 12:05 AM the next day. Verify they appear on the correct calendar dates in Progress tab. Simulate a timezone change (travel).
Pass: Sessions attributed to correct local calendar date at time of logging. Timezone change does not retroactively shift existing session dates.
📱 3. UI Completeness 10 tests ▼
TC-021
All 6 Tabs Reachable and Functional
From app launch, verify each tab (Home, Trainer, Workout, Progress, StrengthLab, Settings) is reachable by tap, loads without error, and shows expected content.
Pass: All 6 tabs reachable in 1 tap from the tab bar. Each renders primary content. No tab shows blank or loading indefinitely.
TC-022
Safe Area Padding on All Screens
On iPhone with Dynamic Island (iPhone 17 Pro) and notch (iPhone 14), verify no content is hidden under the status bar, Dynamic Island, or home indicator area on any screen.
Pass: No text, buttons, or interactive elements obscured by system UI on any screen. Bottom content has sufficient padding above home indicator.
TC-023
Dark Mode Throughout
Set device to Dark Mode. Navigate all 6 tabs and all primary sub-screens. No white flash, no light-mode card, no hardcoded white background in any view.
Pass: Every screen respects dark mode. No hardcoded UIColor.white or Color.white. Color scheme consistent with design system throughout.
TC-024
No Placeholder Text or TODO Strings
Search all visible strings in the app for "TODO", "FIXME", "placeholder", "lorem ipsum", "test string", "coming soon". None should appear in production UI.
Pass: Zero placeholder or development-only strings visible in any production screen during normal navigation.
TC-025
No Empty States Without Content
Test each tab with zero data (fresh install). Verify every tab has a meaningful empty state (message, call-to-action, or illustration). No tab should show a completely blank screen.
Pass: Every empty state communicates value and provides a clear next action. No blank white or black screens with zero content.
TC-026
Navigation Back from All Screens
Navigate into every drill-down screen (exercise detail, session detail, plan detail, settings sub-screens). Verify back navigation works from every screen, ideally with swipe gesture and back button.
Pass: Back navigation from every screen returns to the correct parent. No navigation stack corruption. Swipe-to-go-back works consistently.
TC-027
No Truncated Text on Standard Exercise Names
Check the exercise list and workout view for all exercises with long names (e.g., "Incline Dumbbell Fly", "Romanian Deadlift", "Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press"). Verify no name truncates to "..." inappropriately.
Pass: Exercise names fully visible or ellipsis is intentional and designed. No critical information cut off in any standard layout.
TC-028
Consistent Typography — HONTheme Compliance
Visually audit all screens against DESIGN_SYSTEM.md. Verify font weights, sizes, and colors match the design tokens. No freelanced type styles not in the system.
Pass: All text uses HONTheme type styles. No hardcoded font sizes or colors that deviate from the documented design tokens.
TC-029
Landscape Orientation — No Layout Breaks
Rotate device to landscape on key screens: Active Workout, Home, Progress charts. Verify layout does not break catastrophically (minor suboptimal layout is acceptable if content is still usable).
Pass: No layout elements overlap or disappear in landscape. Content is accessible even if not optimized for landscape. No crash on rotation.
TC-030
Scroll Performance — No Jank on Lists
With 50+ session history, scroll through the Progress tab workout history list. Also scroll through the exercise picker with full exercise database. Verify smooth 60fps scrolling.
Pass: No visible dropped frames during scrolling. No noticeable lag when list has 50+ items. Exercise picker scrolls smoothly through full database.
🎯 4. Experience & Flow 10 tests ▼
TC-031
Onboarding Completes in Under 3 Minutes
Time the full onboarding flow from first launch to home screen. Use a stopwatch. Test with a non-technical user if possible. Include all mandatory steps.
Pass: Onboarding completes in ≤3 minutes for a user who reads each screen carefully. Under 90 seconds for a user who knows what they want.
TC-032
First Workout Completable with Zero Prior Knowledge
Give the app to someone with no prior briefing. Ask them to "log a workout." Time them. Note any moments of confusion or help-seeking.
Pass: User completes first session without help in ≤10 minutes. Finds exercise picker, logs sets, and completes session without verbal guidance.
TC-033
Trainer Generates Plan from Cold Start
Open Trainer tab immediately after completing onboarding (0 sessions logged). Verify a plan or meaningful content is generated. Not a blank screen or error state.
Pass: Trainer tab has usable content within 2 seconds of navigation. Content is relevant to the user's stated goal and equipment.
TC-034
Celebration Fires After First-Ever Completion
Complete the very first workout session on a fresh install. Verify a celebration moment (animation, confetti, milestone message, or equivalent) fires exactly once.
Pass: Visible celebration fires on first session completion. Celebration is proportionate and not over-engineered. Does not repeat on subsequent sessions.
TC-035
Comeback Message Fires at 7+ Day Gap
Seed last session 8 days ago. Open app. Verify a comeback/gap message appears exactly once. Verify it does NOT fire at 6 days. Verify the historyKey is "Short Gap" at 8 days and "Long Gap" at 21+ days.
Pass: Gap message fires at ≥7 days, not before. Correct historyKey phrase bank used. Message fires once per return, not per app open.
TC-036
Feel Selector Appears After Every Session
Complete 5 sessions. Verify the feel/mood selector (post-session rating) appears after each completion. Verify skipping it is possible without penalty. Verify it doesn't appear mid-session.
Pass: Feel selector appears on 100% of session completions. Skippable. Captured data persists and influences readiness on next session.
TC-037
Coaching Narrative Appears After Each Set
Log 10 sets across 3 exercises. Verify coaching narrative text appears after each logged set (or at minimum, is available/accessible after each set). Narrative should be contextually varied.
Pass: Coaching text present after every logged completed set. No two consecutive sets show identical text in the same session. Text is grammatically correct.
TC-038
Progress Page Readable with Under 10 Sessions
Seed exactly 7 sessions (2.5 weeks). Navigate to Progress tab. Every chart, stat, and section should display meaningful content or a clear "not enough data yet" placeholder. No confusing empty charts.
Pass: Progress tab is useful and navigable with 7 sessions. Sparse charts have helpful callouts. Streak and PR sections populated. No crash on small datasets.
TC-039
Cardio Session Completable End-to-End
Start, log, and complete a cardio session (any cardio type) without any strength training context. The entire flow from tap to completion should be independent of the strength logging flow.
Pass: Cardio session completable in <2 minutes of interaction. No dead ends. Session saved and visible in Progress. No dependency on strength training state.
TC-040
Weight Suggestions Appear Within 1 Second
With 5+ sessions logged for an exercise, tap to add that exercise in an active session. Measure time from tap to weight suggestion appearing. Use stopwatch or Xcode instruments.
Pass: Weight suggestion visible ≤1 second after exercise is added to session. Suggestion based on lastPerformanceCache (O(1) lookup, not live scan).
🗣 5. Coaching Quality 8 tests ▼
TC-041
No Generic Filler Phrases
Complete 3 full sessions. Read every coaching narrative phrase. Flag any phrase that could have been generated by a generic fitness app with no context about this user's actual performance.
Pass: Every phrase references or implies knowledge of the user's actual rep/weight/RPE context. Zero phrases that are 100% context-free generic motivational text.
TC-042
No Grammatical Errors in Any Phrase
Read all phrases visible across a 3-session testing sprint. Any grammar errors (subject-verb agreement, tense consistency, punctuation) are a Fail. Pay particular attention to phrase assembly seams where two phrases are concatenated.
Pass: Zero grammatical errors. Assembled phrases (from multiple phrase bank components) read as natural sentences. No dangling words from phrase concatenation.
TC-043
No Phrase Repeats Within Same Session
Log 15 sets across 5 exercises in a single session. Record every coaching phrase shown. Verify no exact phrase repeats within the same session. The pick() rotation mechanism must prevent repeats.
Pass: Zero exact phrase duplicates within a single session. pick() rotation prevents back-to-back identical phrases. UserDefaults rotation state persists correctly.
TC-044
Gap Message Fires Only on Actual Gap
Log sessions on consecutive days. Verify the "Short Gap" or "Long Gap" message does NOT appear when training has been continuous. The historyKey for recent training must be "Normal".
Pass: Gap messages absent on day-after or 2-day-after sessions. historyKey = "Normal" for active users. False positive rate = 0%.
TC-045
Narrative Matches Session Context
Test 3 distinct contexts: (1) comeback after 10-day gap, (2) personal record day, (3) plateau (4th week same weight). Verify the coaching narrative is materially different and contextually appropriate for each.
Pass: Comeback session shows "Short Gap" or "Long Gap" phrasing. PR session shows achievement acknowledgment. Plateau session shows "Stalled" historyKey content. Not generic in any case.
TC-046
RPE Word Chips Map to Correct Values
Tap each RPE chip in the UI. Verify the word label matches the standard RPE scale: 6=Light, 7=Moderate, 8=Hard, 9=Very Hard, 10=Max (or equivalent H.O.N. terminology). Store the saved value and verify it matches the chip selected.
Pass: Each chip stores the correct numeric RPE. Label text is accurate to standard RPE scale language or documented H.O.N. equivalents.
TC-047
Coaching Tone Passes H.O.N. Voice Check
Read 20 coaching phrases across varied sessions. Apply the H.O.N. voice standard: direct, honest, not preachy, not sycophantic, not guilt-inducing, not generic. Flag any phrase that violates tone.
Pass: Zero phrases that are sycophantic ("Amazing job!!" type), preachy ("You really need to rest"), or generic (could appear in any fitness app). All phrases feel like a knowledgeable friend talking to you.
TC-048
Drop Set and To-Failure Modifiers Active
Log a set with isDropCompleted = true. Verify "Drop Set — Completed" phrase bank key fires. Log a set with toFailure = true. Verify "To Failure" connective phrase fires. Test that these modifiers replace the correct phrase slots.
Pass: Drop set modifier replaces weight slot with correct phrase bank key. To-failure modifier replaces trend slot. Both fire only when the respective flag is true.
⚡ 6. Performance 6 tests ▼
TC-049
Tab Switch Under 100ms
With seeded data (30+ sessions), use Xcode Time Profiler to measure render time on tab switch. Swipe between all 6 tabs. No tab should block the main thread for >100ms during a switch.
Pass: All tab switches complete main-thread work in ≤100ms. No visible lag. No spinner or loading state during tab switch.
TC-050
Analytics Refresh Under 500ms Background
Log a new session and observe the refreshAnalytics() cycle. The background thread must complete all cache rebuilds and return to main within 500ms. Use instruments or logging to measure.
Pass: refreshAnalytics() completes in ≤500ms for datasets up to 100 sessions. Token debounce mechanism correctly drops stale results.
TC-051
App Launch Under 2 Seconds (Cold Start)
Kill the app completely. Launch it. Time from tap to fully interactive home screen. Measure 3 times and use average. Include isLoaded transition in the measurement.
Pass: Cold start to interactive home screen ≤2 seconds on iPhone 17 Pro simulator or modern device. No loading spinner after 2s from tap.
TC-052
No Jank on Workout Scroll
In an active workout with 8+ exercises and 5+ sets each, scroll up and down through the active workout view. Use Xcode GPU Frame Capture to verify consistent 60fps rendering.
Pass: Active workout scrolling maintains 60fps. No frame drops visible to the naked eye. No layout recalculations blocking scroll.
TC-053
No ANR or Watchdog Timeout
Run the full app for 30 minutes with active use (logging, tab switching, analytics navigation). Monitor for watchdog terminations in Xcode Console. Check for Main Thread Checker violations.
Pass: Zero watchdog terminations. Zero Main Thread Checker violations in a 30-minute session. All heavy operations on background threads.
TC-054
Memory Under 150MB Typical Session
Use Xcode Memory Gauge during a 30-minute simulated session (logging, analytics viewing, tab switching). Peak memory should not exceed 150MB. Alert if >200MB.
Pass: Typical session memory ≤150MB peak. No memory leak (memory not growing unboundedly across repeated analytics refreshes). exerciseHistoryCache capped at 20 per exercise.
♿ 7. Accessibility 5 tests ▼
TC-055
Dynamic Type — All Text Scales Correctly
Set iOS text size to "Accessibility Extra Large" in Settings > Accessibility > Display & Text Size. Navigate all primary screens. Text must be legible and layouts must not break catastrophically.
Pass: All text scales at Accessibility Extra Large. No text truncated to a single character. No overlapping elements. Core functionality accessible at maximum text size.
TC-056
VoiceOver Labels on All Interactive Elements
Enable VoiceOver. Navigate through active workout logging, the home screen, and settings. Every button, slider, and input must have a descriptive accessibility label. No "Button" without context.
Pass: VoiceOver announces meaningful labels for all interactive elements. No elements announced as just "Button" or "Image". Log buttons clearly describe their function.
TC-057
Minimum 44pt Tap Targets
Use Xcode Accessibility Inspector to measure tap target sizes on all interactive elements. Particular focus on: set log buttons, RPE chips, weight increment/decrement buttons, tab bar items.
Pass: All interactive elements ≥44×44pt tap area. Elements visually smaller than 44pt must have expanded hit areas via .contentShape or padding.
TC-058
Color Not Sole Indicator of State
Review all status indicators, progress states, and alerts. Every state communicated by color must also have a text label, icon, or pattern as a secondary indicator. Critical for colorblind users.
Pass: No information is conveyed by color alone. Pass/fail states, readiness levels, and progress indicators all have non-color secondary cues.
TC-059
Contrast Ratio ≥ 4.5:1 for Body Text
Use Colour Contrast Analyser or Xcode Accessibility Inspector to measure foreground-to-background contrast for all body text. WCAG AA standard requires 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text.
Pass: All body text ≥4.5:1 contrast ratio. All large text (18pt+) ≥3:1. Secondary text (rgba 0.55) must also meet minimum thresholds on its background.

Log 10+ sessions. Navigate to Settings. Find and use "Delete All Data" or equivalent function. Verify all workout data, user profile, and cached analytics are completely removed. App should return to onboarding state.
Pass: All user data removable from within the app. After deletion, app returns to clean state. No orphaned data in any cache or UserDefaults. Deletion is irreversible with confirmation dialog.

Section 3 — Definition of Done

H.O.N. is done and ready to ship when a person with no prior knowledge of the codebase can pick up an iPhone, install the app, complete onboarding in under three minutes, log their first workout without any help, and walk away having received coaching feedback that feels specific to what they just did — and when that experience is rock-solid for the 22 types of real people in our cohort, from the 58-year-old woman worried about osteoporosis to the powerlifter who will fact-check every e1RM calculation, from the night-shift nurse with irregular training patterns to the bad actor trying to crash the app with 999 kg inputs. Done means all 63 test cases are green, zero P0 bugs are open, the crash-free rate on TestFlight exceeds 99.5%, every calculation in the codebase matches the documented formulas to within 0.5%, the coaching narrative never repeats itself in a single session and never sounds like it was written for someone else, the app launches in under 2 seconds, tabs switch in under 100ms, and the entire experience works flawlessly in Airplane Mode. Done does not mean feature-complete — it means the features we ship are complete, correct, honest, and worthy of the H.O.N. name.
